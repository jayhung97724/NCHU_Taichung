Protocol

H2O

Cloning (3A and Gibson Assembly)
  1. PCR
    KOD plus polymerase1 μl
    dNTP2 μl
    10XBuffer2 μl
    Forward Primer1 μl
    Reverse Primer1 μl
    MgSO4 2 μl
    DNA1 μl
    H2O10 μl
  2. Restriction Enzyme Digestion

    Restriction enzyme : EcoRI , XbaI , SpeI , PStI

    NEB restriction enzyme(1μl enzyme can react with 1 μg DNA)
    DNA
    10x Cutsmart buffer2 μl
    Add H2O to 20 μl
  3. Gel extraction THERMO Gel extraction
    1. Excise gel slice containing the DNA fragment using a clean scalpel or razor blade
    2. Put into Eppendorf (remember to keep the gel weight in 100-200mg)
    3. Add equal volume of binding buffer , vortex
    4. Incubate it until all the gel is dissolved in the buffer
    5. Transfer all the solution to the purification column then centrifuge for 15,000rpm, 1min
    6. Discard the flow-through and add 100μl binding buffer then centrifuge for 15,000rpm, 1min
    7. Discard the flow through and add 700μl wash buffer then centrifuge for 15,000rpm, 1min
    8. Discard the flow through and repeat (7) process
    9. Discard the flow through and centrifuge for 15,000rpm, 7 min
    10. Transfer the purification column to the 1.5ml eppendorf
    11. Incubate it in 50-55℃ for 10 min
    12. Add 20μl H2O (prewarm) or Elution buffer into the center of the column and wait for 2-3 min
    13. Centrifuge for 15,000rpm, 7 min
    14. Use nanodrop to quantify the nucleic acids’ concentration
  4. DNA clean up (after cutting, if the residue is in 5-8bp you can use DNA clean up) Genemark DNA clean up kit
    1. Add 3 volumes of binding buffer, mix completely
    2. Transfer the solution into purification column, centrifuge for 1 min in top speed
    3. Discard the flow-through and add 700μl of wash solution, centrifuge for 1 min in top speed
    4. Discard the flow-through and repeat step (3)
    5. Centrifuge for 7 min in top speed
    6. Incubate in 50-55℃
    7. Add rewarmed H2O or elution buffer into the center of the column, wait for 2-3 min
    8. Centrifuge for 7 min in top speed
    9. Use nanodrop to quantify the nucleic acids’ concentration
  5. Ligation Promega T4 ligase
    1. Calcμlate the vector and insert ‘s ratio $${ng\ of\ vector\times\ kb\ size\ of\ insert\times molar\ ratio\ of\ insert\over kb\ size\ of\ vector\ vector} = ng\ of\ insert$$vector : insert = 1 : 3 is quite optimal
    2. Add 10X buffer
    3. Add T4 ligase
    4. Add H2O till 20μl in total volume
    5. Put it in 4℃ overnight or 22℃ for 3 hr
  6. Transformation Use DH5alpha E.coli comercial competent cell
    1. Add 10μl DNA into 40 μl competent cell
    2. Put it on the ice for 20-30min
    3. 42℃ heat shock
    4. Put it back onto the ice for 3 min
    5. Add 250 SOC medium and 37℃ incubate for 1 hr
    6. Plate all of the transformation and equally distribute it onto a 10 cm LB agar plate containing the appropriate antibiotic
    7. 37℃ incubate overnight
  7. Gibson assembly
    1. 2–3 Fragment Assembly DNA 0.02-0.5 pmols, Gibson Assembly Master Mix 10μl, Add H2O to 20μl in total volume
    2. Incubate in 50℃ for 15 min when 2-3 fragment assembly, store on ice or in -20℃ for transformation
